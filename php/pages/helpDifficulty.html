Challenge difficulties

A challenge's difficulty determines the number of points earned when completed (the more difficult a challenge is, the more points earned). It is therefore important to choose it carefully.

Here is an approximate difficulty scale for reference. Try to stick to it for your challenges.

  • An easy challenge has to be doable for an absolute beginner.
  • A medium challenge is typically difficult for a beginner but easy for an experimented player.
  • A difficult challenge would be challenging for an experimented player but doable in several trials.
  • An extreme challenge will require an experimented player to try-hard.
  • An impossible challenge will require experimented players to try for several hours, or even days before succeeding.

Note that challenges are moderated before publication, so you have no interest over-evaluating the difficulty of your challenges.
